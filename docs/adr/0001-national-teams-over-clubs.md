# National teams over clubs

The Tournament uses the 32 strongest national Teams (shown with flags) instead of
club teams (shown with crests/logos). Club crests and names are registered
trademarks, which would create legal and asset-sourcing risk for a free, publicly
hosted app; national flags are free to use and render natively as Unicode emoji.
The number 32 also matches the classic World Cup knockout format, so national teams
fit the format with no downside. Revisit only if a licensing arrangement makes club
branding viable.
